Love Walking

Walking is a low impact exercise that puts less stress on your joints. So it’s great if you have back, hip, knee or ankle problems. Low impact exercise like

walking
can be very effective too. You’ll expend about the same amount of energy walking three kilometres as you would running the same distance. Walking will just take you a little longer.
